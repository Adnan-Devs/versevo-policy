Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2025

Thank you for using Versevo. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data and privacy when you use our poetry app.

1. No Account or Sign-In Required

Versevo does not require users to create an account or sign in. We do not collect personal user data like email, name, or login credentials.

2. Data Sources

The content displayed in the app, such as poems and categories, is fetched from Google Firebase Firestore. This data is static and curated by the app developer.

3. User Data

We do not collect or store any personal information. No user-generated content or tracking is implemented in the current version of the app.

4. Ads

As of now, the app does not contain ads. However, in the future, Versevo may display ads to support development and maintenance. This will be updated in the policy when implemented.

5. Sharing

Users are allowed and encouraged to share poems from Versevo freely. The content is meant to inspire and be spread creatively.

6. Open Source

The Versevo app is open-source and will be available on GitHub for transparency and community collaboration.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Please check back periodically for updates.

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact us via the GitHub repository or support channels listed in the app.

Last updated: May 2025
